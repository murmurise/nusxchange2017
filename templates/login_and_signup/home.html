About NUSXchange Quick Start

What we do

National University of Singapore (NUS) hosts approximately 1.400 international students from 180 universities across the globe join the vibrant student community for at least one semester exchange. NUSXchange is here to help bridges the gap between the two groups efficiently and effectively by simply provide a way to find friends.

Why you would love NUSXchange

Real

Don't worry about fake identity anymore! All users in NUSXchange are registered NUS students verified with their matric number.

Simple & Beautiful

With intuitive minimalist design, NUSXchange simply find you friends tailored to your profile!

Free of Charge

NUSXchange is, of course, free of charge. FOREVER.

Growing

NUSXchange is still a young service with the room for many more functionalities. Contribute your thoughts and be credited for the next awesome feature!
